How to Cancel Booking?

To cancel a booking, follow these steps: 1. Check the cancellation policy of your booking to see if you are eligible for a refund. This information can usually be found on the booking confirmation or on the website where you made the reservation. 2. Contact the company or establishment where you made the booking, either by phone or email, and provide them with your booking details. 3. Request to cancel your booking and ask for confirmation of the cancellation and any refund that you may be entitled to. 4. Follow any additional steps that may be required for the cancellation process, such as returning any tickets or vouchers that you have received. 5. Make sure to keep records of all communication and confirmations related to the cancellation in case you need them in the future. It's important to note that cancellation policies can vary depending on the company or establishment, so it's always a good idea to read the terms and conditions carefully before making a reservation.

When Will I get the possession?

The timeline for getting possession of a property in real estate can vary depending on various factors such as the type of property, location, and terms of the purchase agreement. Here are some general timelines to keep in mind: New construction: If you are purchasing a property that is still under construction, the builder will provide you with an estimated completion date. You can expect to get possession of the property within a few weeks to a few months after the completion date, depending on the builder's policies. Resale property: If you are purchasing a resale property, the timeline for getting possession can vary based on the seller's availability to move out, any repairs or renovations required, and the terms of the purchase agreement. Typically, you can expect to get possession of the property within a few weeks to a few months after the closing date.
